Our Story · 我们的故事

蜀府——地道川味冒菜与麻辣香锅。柜台自选食材，可选麻辣汤煮（冒菜）或干锅炒制（麻辣香锅），辣度麻度随你。

Shufu — “the Shu household” — cooks Sichuan maocai and mala dry pot. Pick your ingredients at the counter; they simmer them in numbing-spicy broth (冒菜) or wok-toss them dry (麻辣香锅), dialed to your heat.
